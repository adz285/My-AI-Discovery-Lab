AI-Ready Product Infrastructure

We rebuild the machine-readable layer so AI systems can reliably interpret your products, variants, attributes, and constraints. This is infrastructure work — not “SEO tactics”.

AI systems don’t just crawl pages. They infer meaning. When your structured signals are incomplete, inconsistent, or ambiguous, products become harder to retrieve and recommend.

Structured Product Data (JSON-LD)

Validate and strengthen JSON-LD so products resolve as clear entities with consistent identifiers and attributes.

Variant-Level Offer Structure

Map each variant to its own Offer for accurate pricing, availability, URLs, and SKU/GTIN continuity.

Attributes & Spec Modelling

Convert key attributes into machine-readable properties so AI can compare, filter, and reason about your catalog.

FAQ Structured Data (Where Relevant)

Add FAQPage schema where it clarifies buyer questions and exposes consistent Q/A structure to AI systems.

SEO is indexing. AI is interpretation.

Traditional search finds pages. AI-driven discovery tries to understand products. That means your store needs structured signals that reasoning systems can trust: entities, attributes, variant logic, constraints, and consistent identifiers.

This applies across e-commerce — Shopify, WooCommerce, headless, and custom storefronts. Supplements are a common case because the gap is obvious (ingredients, dosing, claims), but the same structural issues exist for apparel, electronics, cosmetics, home goods, and more.

  • Default platform schema is typically minimal and inconsistent across templates.
  • Attributes/specs are often plain text, which reduces machine comparability.
  • Variants frequently collapse into a single Offer, causing ambiguity in price and availability.
  • FAQ data is rarely structured, so Q/A extraction becomes unreliable.
Infrastructure, not hype

This is the machine-readable layer your platform rarely ships well by default. We diagnose it and give you the blueprint to rebuild it cleanly — schema type alignment, per-variant offers, computable attributes, and structured Q/A where it matters.
